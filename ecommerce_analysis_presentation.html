Step 5 · Insights & recommendations
What the data
demands we do.
Critical — funnel
88.84% view without adding to cart
Biggest revenue leak. Poor PDP UX, missing social proof, and price anchoring keep 2.68M users from engaging. Fix the product page first.
High — retention
75.6% of users never return after W+1
No re-engagement mechanism detected. A Day-3 / Day-7 personalised push campaign is the single highest-ROI retention investment.
Positive signal
37.86% repeat purchase rate
Strong product-market fit. Champions average $1,733 revenue each. A VIP loyalty tier could convert 137K Potential Loyalists to this level.
Strategic
84.07% bounce rate — view only
High bounce signals discovery friction. Weekend revenue is only 25.7% of weekly — promotions on weekends could shift this split significantly.
